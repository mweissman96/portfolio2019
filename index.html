A New Project...

The first complete Unreal Tournament 4 map. After much time spent learning Unreal Engine 3 without producing anything of professional quality, I set my sights on seeing a full-fledged map through from start to finish. Though I continue to work on updates behind the scenes, Legacy stands as my greatest work in Unreal Engine 4 to date.

Voss Reborn

A reference both to the Smash series and to the lack of Sci-Fi elements common to Unreal Tournament maps, TechThis is designed to provide seasoned players with a little bit more of an environmental challenge. It is a smaller map with a generally uncomplicated layout, built from start to finish mostly within a single weekend. This map is designed to be more challenging than other maps, requiring the player to make use of advanced techniques such as wall-dodging and sliding. TechThis was built primarily as a proof-of-concept, demonstrating several unusual material types and a custom damage type.

Legacy

TwilightDeck is the unlikely marriage between Unreal Tournament and Spyro the Dragon. This map is a reimagination of Deck, an Unreal Tournament classic. Replacing UT pickups with gems and repurposing rooms to be outdoors are just some of the cool features of this map. While it may not be as ambitious of a crossover as Infinity War, this map is sure to awaken nostalgia in the hearts of OG Spyro fans.
