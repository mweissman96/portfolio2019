About Me

Matthew Weissman is a game and level designer who has been working at his craft for over 10 years. He is mostly self-taught, and also pursues other related interests such as programming and music composition. He is inspired by the majestic, colorful worlds of Spyro the Dragon, as well as the mechanical complexity and rich lore of Star Wars: The Old Republic.

His work primarily focuses on light, vibrant color, and non-solid materials. He enjoys exploring the gap between fantasy and realism, and between science fiction and realism. His efforts are focused in Unreal Engine 4. In his current project, unnamed as of yet, he is laying the groundwork for a full-fledged, AAA-scope game. He currently resides in San Ramon, where he recently moved after getting married over the summer.
